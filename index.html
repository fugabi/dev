WEB/DEV

An intuitive, efficient, and beautiful website can set you apart and take you to the next level. That’s what I am here to help you do.

I work in a range of platforms, from content management systems such as Wordpress and Ghost to static site generators such as Hugo, Jekyll, and 11ty. Lately, I have been exploring utility-first frameworks like Tailwind, which are breathing new life into the CSS landscape.

Whether you want to update an existing website or start one from scratch, I’m ready to work with you every step of the way.
